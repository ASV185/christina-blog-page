My Story

Hi, I'm Christina! Jesus Christ is the most important part of my life. I grew up in El Paso, TX, where familia and food are essential. When I'm not working as a registered nurse, you can find me traveling and exploring the great outdoors.

"For I know the plans that I have for you, declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future." Jeremiah 29:11

The Lord has placed it on my heart to use my love of traveling to serve Him on a six-month DTS mission trip with YWAM. So far, I have traveled to Hawaii, New York City, and Atlanta. Join me by reading my blogs as I take my DTS mission trip with YWAM.
